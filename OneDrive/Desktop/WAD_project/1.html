Learn More About Organic Farming

What is Organic Farming?

Organic farming is a method of agriculture that avoids the use of synthetic chemicals, focusing on maintaining ecological balance and biodiversity.

Benefits of Organic Farming

Organic farming practices improve soil health, reduce pollution, and promote biodiversity. It also helps in producing healthier crops free from harmful pesticides.

How to Transition to Organic Farming

Switching to organic farming requires understanding the ecosystem, using natural fertilizers, and choosing organic pest control methods.
